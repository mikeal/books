真筆本 (Shinpitsu-bon)

入宋傳法沙門道元 撰

原夫道本圓通。爭假修證。宗乘自在。何費功夫。況乎全體逈出塵埃。孰信拂拭之手段。大都不離當處。豈用修行之脚頭。然而毫釐有差天地懸隔。違順纔起紛然失心。須知歴劫輪迴還因擬議之一念。塵世迷道復由商量之無休。欲超向上之徹底。唯 解直下之承當。直饒誇會豐悟。獲瞥地之智通。得道明心。擧衝天之志氣。雖有入頭之量。尚缺出身之路。矧彼釋迦老子之爲生知。已在六年端坐之跡。達磨大師之傳心印。更貽九歳面壁之蹤。古聖既然。今人盍辨。所以 ニ 翻尋言逐語之鮮行。須迴光返照之退歩。自然身心脱落。本來面目現前。欲得恁麼。急務坐禪。


原夫道本圓通。
Original root path [is already] perfectly complete.

爭假修證
Why rely on practice (sādhana) [and/or] experience?

宗乘自在。
[Our] essential teaching is natural liberation.

何費功夫。
What need for "skillful technique" (gōng fū)?


況乎全體逈出塵埃。
[This] entire body wholly transcends "dust of the world."

孰信拂拭之手段。
Who would then believe in methods of purification?

大都不離當處。
Fundamentally not separated from this place.

豈用修行之脚頭。
Why practice the preliminaries?


然而毫釐有差天地懸隔。
Nevertheless, infinitesimal differences will separate heaven and earth.

違順纔起紛然失心。
As favorable and unfavorable conditions arise this mind is lost in confusion.

須知歴劫輪迴還因擬議之一念。
[One] must know samsara across countless kalpas as caused by conceptualization in a single thought-moment (kṣaṇa).

塵世迷道復由商量之無休。
[This] world of dust loses the path by deliberation without end.


欲超向上之徹底。
Wishing to transcend upward to completion,

唯 解直下之承當。
Directly realize and accept below,

直饒誇會豐悟。
Even when boasting of abundant realizations,

獲瞥地之智通。
Gain a fleeting moment of intuitive wisdom.


得道明心。
Attaining path [is] awakening [this] mind.

擧衝天之志氣。
Will power that lifts to reach the heavens,

雖有入頭之量。
May have potential in the beginning,

尚缺出身之路。
Still lacks a path to leave [this] body.


矧彼釋迦老子之爲生知。
Moreover, consider Buddha Shakyamuni, naturally wise,

已在六年端坐之跡。
Yet situated in upright sitting for six years.

達磨大師之傳心印。
Great Master Bodhidharma, who transmitted the mind-seal,

更貽九歳面壁之蹤。
Additionally left nine years of marks facing the wall.


古聖既然。
Ancient sages were like this,

今人盍辨。
Why should people today not discern this?

所以 ニ 翻尋言逐語之鮮行。
Thus, to seek (vicāra) words and phrases rarely leads to practice.

須迴光返照之退歩。
Turning light reflects illumination.


自然身心脱落。
Naturally, body and mind drop off,

本來面目現前。
Original face clearly manifests.

欲得恁麼。
Wishing to attain this,

急務坐禪。
Urgently practice zazen.


夫 參禪者。靜室宜焉。飮飡節矣。乃放捨諸縁休息萬事。不思善惡。莫管是非。停心意識之運轉。止念想觀之測量。正坐之時。厚敷坐物。上用蒲團。然後結跏趺坐。 或半跏趺坐。謂結跏趺坐。先以右足安左上。左足安右上。半跏趺坐。但以左足壓右矣。寛繋衣帶可令齊整。次右手安左足上。左掌安右掌上。以兩大拇指面相拄。 乃正身端坐。不得左側右傾前躬後仰。要令耳與肩對鼻與臍對。舌掛上腭唇齒相著。目須常開。身相既定氣息亦調。念起即覺。覺之即失。久久忘縁自成一片。此坐 禪之要術也。謂坐禪則大安樂法門也。若得此意。自然四大輕安。精神爽利。正念分明。法味資神。寂然清樂。日用天眞也。已能發明。可謂。如龍得水。似虎靠 山。當知。正念現前。昏散曷到。若從坐起。徐徐動身。安祥而起。不應卒暴。於一切時護持定力。參究之超上關無本可據證。放之被自礙。所以未留乃道之十成 也。


夫 參禪者。
Now, for those who engage in zazen,

靜室宜焉。
A quiet room is appropriate,

飮飡節矣。
And moderate food and drink.

乃放捨諸縁休息萬事。
Thus, release all conditions and cease every matter.


不思善惡。
Do not think good or bad.

莫管是非。
Do not manage right or wrong.

停心意識之運轉。
Stop revolving mind consciousness.

止念想觀之測量。
Cease measuring thoughts and perceptions.


正坐的时候。  
When sitting properly.  

厚敷坐物。  
Spread out a zabuton.  

上用蒲团。  
Place a zafu on top.  

然后结跏趺坐。  
Then sit in full lotus.  

或半跏趺坐。
Or sit in half lotus.


謂結跏趺坐。
This is called full lotus:

先以右足安左上。
First place the right foot on top of the left,

左足安右上。
Then place the left foot on top of the right.


半跏趺坐。  
This is called half lotus:  

但以左足壓右矣。  
Just press left foot on right knee.  

寛繋衣帶可令齊整。  
Loosen clothing and belt to make it orderly.  

次右手安左足上。  
Next, place right hand to stabilize left foot.  


左掌安右掌上。  
Place the left palm on the right palm.  

以兩大拇指面相拄。  
Both thumb surfaces touch to support each other.  

乃正身端坐。  
Then straighten the body to sit upright.  

不得左側右傾前躬後仰。  
Do not lean left, tilt right, bend forward, or lean back.  


要令耳與肩對鼻與臍對。
Ensure ears align with shoulders and nose with navel.

舌掛上腭唇齒相著。
Hang tongue against the upper palate with lips and teeth resting together.

目須常開。
Eyes must remain open.

身相既定氣息亦調。
Once body stablizes, breathing also harmonizes.


念起即覺。
As thoughts arise become aware at once.

覺之即失。
Awareness of it dissolves it at once.

久久忘縁自成一片。
In time, lose sight of conditions naturally to become unifed.

此坐 禪之要術也。
This is the essential method of zazen.


謂坐禪則大安樂法門也。
Zazen is therefor the Great Dharma door of ease and joy.

若得此意。
If one grasps this meaning,

自然四大輕安。
Naturally the four great elements become subtle at ease.

精神爽利。
Awareness clear and sharp.


正念分明。  
Right mindfulness distinctly clear.

法味資神。  
Taste of the Dharma supports consciousness.

寂然清樂。  
In silence there is pure joy.

日用天眞也。  
In daily practice, natural truth.


已能發明。
Already able to manifest clearly.

可謂。
It can be said,

如龍得水。
Like a dragon receives water,

似虎靠山。
Like a tiger depends on a mountain.


當知。
You should know,

正念現前。
Rigth mindfulness manifesting clearly.

昏散曷到。
How could confusion arise?


若從坐起。
When rising from sitting.

徐徐動身。
Move body very gently.

安祥而起。
Rise peacefully,

不應卒暴。
Not abrubtly or abrasively.


於一切時護持定力。
At all times protect concentration power.

參究之超上關無本可據證。
To investigate transcendental gates, no foundation can be depended on as proof.

放之被自礙。
Releasing it becomes a self-imposed hindrance.

所以未留乃道之十成也。
Thus, leaving nothing is complete perfection of Path.


誠 禪定一門最爲高勝。先以十分之會擧。次轉一半之證來。只在此法。拈花破顏禮拜得髓。皆承他之恩力而獲大自在者也。學般若菩薩。詎不隨順者乎。嘗觀。超凡越 聖必假靜縁。坐脱立亡能任定力。況復指竿針鎚之轉機。拂拳棒喝之證契。未是思量分別之所能解也。豈爲神通修證之所能知也。可爲聲色之外威儀。那非知見之前 軌則者歟。然則不論上知下愚。莫簡利人鈍者。放下六根。見轉全道。不生一念坐斷十方。凡其自界他方佛法本無異法。西天東地祖門遂開五門。等持佛印。各檀宗 風。唯務單傳。直指專事。翻身迴頭雖謂千差萬別。但喜歸程祥參。何忘却自家之坐床謾去來他國之塵境。若錯一歩。當面蹉過。既得人身之應會。莫虚度光陰。必 憶佛道之當行。誰浪樂石火。加以形質如草露。運命似電光。倏忽便空。須臾即失。冀其參學高流久習摸象勿怪眞龍。早向直指端的之正道。速成絶學無爲之眞人。 方遵百丈之規繩。遍通少林之消息。莫勞拂耳之風。更驚撃舌之響耶。但能正開自寶藏受用使如意。

誠 禪定一門最爲高勝。  
Indeed, dhyāna-samādhi is the most superior single method.  

先以十分之會擧。  
First, bring forth ten measures of understanding.  

次轉一半之證來。  
Next, turn half of that realization.  

只在此法。  
It all resides solely in this Dharma.  


拈花破颜礼拜得髓。  
Holding a flower, open a smile, and paying homage, attains the essence.

皆承他之恩力而获大自在者也。  
All receive his kindness, power, and great liberation.

学般若菩萨。  
Learning Bodhisattva wisdom,

詎不随顺者乎。  
How could one not follow along?


尝观。
Having observed:

超凡越圣必假静缘。
To surpass ordinary and transcend sacred, one must depends on tranquil conditions.

坐脱立亡能任定力。
Sitting to release, standing to die, employ the power of concentration.

况复指竿针锤之转机。
Moreover, direct turning moments as you would use poles, needles, or hammers.


拂拳棒喝之證契。  
Brushing away fist and stick bangs, realization and agreement arise.

未是思量分別之所能解也。  
Not by contemplation and discrimination can they be understood.  

豈爲神通修證之所能知也。  
Nor by magical powers or cultivation can they be known.  

可爲聲色之外威儀。  
They manifest as conduct beyond sights and sounds.


那非知見之前 軌則者歟。  
That such knowledge and perception should proceed a rule?

然則不論上知下愚。  
Then, regardless of the wise or foolish,

莫簡利人鈍者。  
Do not distinguish between sharp or dull people,

放下六根。  
Let go of the six sense faculties.


见转全道。
Seeing entire path turning.

不生一念坐断十方。
Not a single thought arises, cutting off the ten directions.

凡其自界他方佛法本无异法。
In essence, one's own realm of self and Buddha's Dharma are originally with differentiated Dharma.

西天东地祖门遂开五门。
From the Western Heaven to the Eastern Earth, the ancestral gate opens five doors.


等持佛印。
Samādhi is Buddha Mudra.

各檀宗 風。
Each sect gives their style.

唯務單傳。
Only focused on a single transmission.

直指專事。
Directly pointing to an exclusive practice.


翻身迴頭雖謂千差萬別。  
Turning body around is said to involve a thousand descriminations.

但喜歸程祥參。  
Yet I am happy on the return to the path and seek auspiciousness.

何忘却自家之坐床謾去來他國之塵境。  
How could I forget my own medidation seat, carelessly coming and going to dusty realms of other lands,

若錯一歩。  
If I err by just one step.


当面蹉过。  
Face-to-face, yet overlooked.

既得人身之应会。  
Having gained the opportunity of human life,

莫虚度光阴。  
Do not waste time.

必忆佛道之当行。  
Surely remember the practice of the Buddhist path.


誰浪樂石火。
Who reckelessly takes pleasure in fire from a stone?

加以形質如草露。
And regards form and substance as grass and dew?

運命似電光。
Life is like a flash of lightning.

倏忽便空。
Suddenly it is gone, leaving emptiness (sunyata).


須臾即失。  
In an instant, it is lost.

冀其參學高流久習摸象勿怪眞龍。  
Hoping they study with the noble streams, long habituated to touching the elephant, and do not misjudge the true dragon.

早向直指端的之正道。  
Quickly aim directly for the straight and proper path.

速成絶學無爲之眞人。  
Swiftly become one who is free from study, a true person of non-action.


方遵百丈之規繩。
Thus, follow Baizhang's rules.

遍通少林之消息。
Widely understand the movements of Shaolin.

莫勞拂耳之風。
Do not bother with wind that grazes your ears.

更驚撃舌之響耶。
Nor be startled by the clamor of tongues.


但能正開自寶藏受用使如意。
As long as you can properly unlock the storehouse of treasure within, you can make use of it as you wish.


天福元年中元日書于觀音導利院
Written on the Mid-Autumn Festival of the first year of Tianfu, at the Guanyin Daoli Monastery.
